About Us

Blast Industrial Engineering is a company specialized in complete and innovative solutions for the industrial sector. With a focus on technical excellence, innovation, and sustainability, we offer services that drive operational efficiency and deliver superior results to our clients.

Frequently Asked Questions

Check out the most common questions about our services and operations.

What are the main services offered by your company?

We provide industrial engineering services, preventive and corrective maintenance, assembly of metal structures, equipment installation, specialized welding, industrial piping, and on-site technical assistance.

Do you serve only large industries or also medium-sized companies?

We serve companies of all sizes. We adapt our solutions according to the complexity, needs, and scale of each client, whether in heavy industry or smaller facilities.

Do you offer preventive maintenance contracts?

Yes. We work with preventive and corrective maintenance contracts, with schedules defined according to the criticality of the equipment, ensuring greater operational reliability and reducing unplanned downtime.

Does your company supply materials and spare parts?

Yes. We supply materials, components, and spare parts according to the technical specifications required by the client, ensuring the quality and compatibility of all items used in the maintenance and assembly processes.

How can I request a quote?

To request a quote, you can contact us through our website, email, or phone. Provide us with as much information as possible about your project or needs, and we will get back to you with a customized proposal.

Do you provide training for teams?

Yes, we offer technical training to qualify teams in operation, maintenance, and industrial safety.
